## Usage Notes
- Do this every Friday or Saturday (end of sprint week)
- Keep it private if you want—the value is in the reflection, not the sharing
- Be brutally honest. Founders who lie to themselves in retros don't learn.
- Review the last 3-4 retros before group office hours—patterns emerge
- If the same thing keeps showing up in "What Didn't Go Well," it's a systemic issue that needs a different approach
