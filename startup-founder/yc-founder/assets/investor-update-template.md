## Usage Notes
- Send on the same date every month (e.g., 1st of each month)
- Send to all investors regardless of whether they can help right now—you're building a long-term relationship
- Be honest about challenges. Investors can't help if they don't know the problems.
- Keep it under 500 words. Investors skim. Make the key numbers impossible to miss.
- Include a specific ask. The most successful founder-investor relationships are reciprocal.
- Reply individually to any investor who responds
