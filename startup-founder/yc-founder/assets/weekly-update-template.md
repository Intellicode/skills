# YC Weekly Update Template

Use this template for your weekly group updates and partner check-ins.

---

## Week [X] Update — [Company Name]

### 📊 Key Metrics

| Metric | This Week | Last Week | Change |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| [Primary metric] | [X] | [X] | [+/-X%] |
| [Secondary metric] | [X] | [X] | [+/-X%] |
| [Third metric] | [X] | [X] | [+/-X%] |

Choose 2-4 metrics that best reflect your business. Common options:
- Revenue (MRR, weekly revenue)
- Active users (DAU, WAU, MAU)
- Growth rate (WoW %, MoM %)
- Retention (D7, D30, monthly)
- User conversations completed

---

### 🚀 What We Shipped

1. **[Feature/change 1]** — One sentence on what and why
2. **[Feature/change 2]** — One sentence on what and why
3. **[Feature/change 3]** — One sentence on what and why

List concrete things you built, shipped, or changed. Not "we worked on X"—say "we shipped X" or "we tested X with Y users."

---

### 🧠 What We Learned

1. **[Insight 1]** — What you learned and how (user conversation, experiment, data)
2. **[Insight 2]** — What you learned and how

This is the most important section. Be honest about surprises and disappointments, not just confirmations.

---

### 🚧 Blockers / Help Needed

1. **[Blocker]** — What's slowing you down and what kind of help you need

---

### 🎯 Next Week's Focus

1. **[Top priority]** — The single most important thing you'll accomplish
2. **[Secondary priority]** — Second most important

Max 2-3 priorities. If everything is a priority, nothing is.

---

### 🤝 Asks for the Group

- [Specific ask 1: intro, feedback, beta tester, etc.]
- [Specific ask 2]

Be specific. "Can anyone intro me to the VP of Engineering at Stripe?" is better than "looking for intros."

---

## Usage Notes
- Update this every Friday before group office hours
- Share with your group partner and batchmates
- Keep it honest. Bad weeks happen. The update is for accountability, not self-promotion.
- Track your metrics in a spreadsheet over time to see trends
